Powerlifting

Different from Olympic weightlifting, powerlifting is 3 main lifts...

  • Squat
  • Bench
  • Deadlift

The main purpose is to use technique and strength to move as much weight as possible in those 3 lifts. It's easy to pick up, but technical enough to keep it interesting. Powerlifting also helps develop a strong foundation of strength and health.
